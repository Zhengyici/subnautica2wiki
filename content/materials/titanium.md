# Titanium

Titanium is a basic, raw resource found in Subnautica 2. Titanium can be found in a single item form, a cluster players have to break with their hand or any tool, or a big cluster where a Sonic Resonator or Feedback Resonator is needed. Titanium can be farmed with the Metal Farm

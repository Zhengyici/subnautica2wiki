# Conduit Crystal

Conduit Crystal is a rare, late game, raw resource found in Subnautica 2. Conduit Crystal can be found in the big cluster form, needing a Sonic Resonator or Feedback Resonator to obtain. Players can observe electrical charges coming off the Conduit Crystals.

Conduit Crystal can be farmed with the Metal Farm.

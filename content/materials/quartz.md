# Quartz

thumb|Picture of Quartz used in the Databank Entry|338x338px
Quartz is a basic, raw resource found in Subnautica 2. Most commonly found in coral domes. Quartz can be found in a single item form, a cluster players have to break with their hand or any tool, or a big cluster where a Sonic Resonator or Feedback Resonator is needed.

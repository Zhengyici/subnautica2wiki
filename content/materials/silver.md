# Silver

thumb|Picture of Silver used in the Databank Entry
Silver is a basic, raw resource found in Subnautica 2. Most commonly found in caves. Silver can be found in a single item form, a cluster players have to break with their hand or any tool, or a big cluster where a Sonic Resonator or Feedback Resonator is needed. 

Silver can be farmed with the Metal Farm.

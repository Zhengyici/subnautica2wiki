# Gold

Gold is a mid game, raw resource found in Subnautica 2. Goldis only found in hot areas, such as the biome players have dubbed the Graveyard, and is also found in areas with heat vents. Gold can be found in the big cluster form, needing a Sonic Resonator or Feedback Resonator to obtain.

Gold can be farmed with the Metal Farm.

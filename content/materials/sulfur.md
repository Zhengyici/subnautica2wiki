# Sulfur

Sulfur is a basic, raw resource found in Subnautica 2. Sulfur is only found in hot areas, such as the biome players have dubbed the Graveyard, and is also found in areas with heat vents. Sulfur can be found in a single item form, a cluster players have to break with their hand or any tool, or a big cluster where a Sonic Resonator or Feedback Resonator is needed. ## Databank Entry

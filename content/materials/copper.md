# Copper

thumb|Picture of Copper used in the Databank Entry
Copper is a basic, raw resource found in Subnautica 2. Most commonly found in caves. Copper can be found in a single item form, a cluster players have to break with their hand or any tool, or a big cluster where a Sonic Resonator or Feedback Resonator is needed.

== Databank Entry ==

# Salt

Salt is a basic, raw resource found in Subnautica 2. Salt can be found in a single item form, a cluster players have to break with their hand or any tool, or a big cluster where a Sonic Resonator or Feedback Resonator is needed.

Salt can be farmed with the Metal Farm.

# Deepwing Brooder

The '''Deepwing Brooder''' is a [[Leviathan Class Organisms|Leviathan class]] fauna in ''[[{{topic}}]]''.

It can be found slowly circling the outer edge of the map.

The creature will often release a rain of eggs. Most eggs will vanish when the player gets close, but 1% of the eggs will look different, and can be grabbed by the player, called Deepwing Egg Clump. It is easiest to see the different egg clump from a distance.

## Overview

The **Deepwing Brooder** is a Leviathan class fauna . It can be found slowly circling the outer edge of the map. The creature will often release a rain of eggs. Most eggs will vanish when the player gets close, but 1% of the eggs will look different, and can be grabbed by the player, called Deepwing Egg Clump.

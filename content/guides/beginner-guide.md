# Subnautica 2 Beginner's Guide

Welcome to Subnautica 2! This guide will help you survive your first hours on Planet Zezura.

## First Steps

1. **Repair your Lifepod** — Fix the radio and other systems first
2. **Gather basic resources** — Collect Titanium, Copper, Quartz, and Rubber
3. **Build a Scanner** — 2x Titanium + 2x Quartz + 1x Basic Battery. This is your most important tool
4. **Build a Survival Multitool** — 3x Titanium
5. **Watch your O2** — Always keep an eye on your oxygen gauge

## Essential First Crafts

| Item | Recipe | Why |
|------|--------|-----|
| Scanner | 2x Titanium, 2x Quartz, 1x Basic Battery | Scan everything to unlock blueprints |
| Basic Fins | 2x Rubber, 2x Fiber | Swim faster |
| Standard Air Tank | 2x Titanium, 1x Rubber, 2x Silver | Stay underwater longer |
| Flashlight | 2x Titanium, 1x Quartz, 1x Basic Battery | Explore dark areas |
| Habitat Builder | 2x Titanium, 1x Glass, 1x Basic Battery, 1x Copper Wire | Build your first base |

## Key Tips

- **Scan everything** — The Scanner unlocks blueprints for new items
- **Build a base early** — Even a single room gives you storage and safety
- **Explore wrecks** — Scanning fragments unlocks critical blueprints
- **Don't ignore the radio** — It leads you to key story points
- **Save often** — The game doesn't always autosave
- **Watch for Leviathans** — Listen for their roars and avoid their territory

## Co-op Tips (New to Subnautica 2!)

Subnautica 2 supports 4-player co-op for the first time:
- Share resources and divide tasks
- One player can distract creatures while another scans
- Build bases together
- Explore different biomes simultaneously

## Resource Priority Guide

**Early Game (0-2 hours):** Titanium, Copper, Quartz, Rubber, Fiber
**Mid Game (2-10 hours):** Silver, Gold, Lead, Lithium, Conduit Crystal
**Late Game (10+ hours):** Troilite, Atacamite, Celestine, Diamond
